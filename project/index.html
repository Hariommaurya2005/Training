Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk Powder
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Prepare sugar syrup and keep it warm.
  2. Make a dough with milk powder and ghee.
  3. Fry small balls until golden brown.
  4. Dip in sugar syrup and serve hot.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder
  • Condensed Milk
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee and add milk powder.
  2. Stir and cook until thick.
  3. Pour into a tray and let it set.
  4. Cut into pieces and serve.
